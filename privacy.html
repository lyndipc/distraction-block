Privacy Policy for Distraction Block

Last updated: March 31, 2025

Overview

Distraction Block is a browser extension designed to help you stay focused by blocking distracting websites. We take your privacy seriously and have designed this extension to respect your data.

Data Collection

Distraction Block does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All of your settings (including your list of blocked websites) are stored locally in your browser using the browser's storage API.

Information Usage

The only information stored by this extension is:

  • Your list of blocked websites
  • Whether blocking is currently enabled or disabled

This information is only used to provide the core functionality of the extension and never leaves your device.

Third-party Services

Distraction Block does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking technologies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with the updated extension.
